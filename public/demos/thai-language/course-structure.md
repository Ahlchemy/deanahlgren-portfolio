# Thai Language Course Structure

## Overall Design
- Single-page scrollable design
- Thai-inspired color theme (gold, saffron orange, emerald green)
- Buddhist and tropical visual elements
- Interactive elements with embedded audio
- Mini-tests at the end of each section

## Sections

### 1. Introduction
- Welcome message
- Brief overview of Thai language and its importance
- How to use this course
- Learning objectives

### 2. Thai Alphabet Basics
- Introduction to Thai script
- Consonants (grouped by similar sounds)
- Vowels and vowel combinations
- Tone marks
- Numbers
- Interactive pronunciation guide with audio
- Mini-test: Match characters to sounds

### 3. Essential Thai Pronunciation
- The five tones of Thai
- Common pronunciation challenges
- Listening exercises
- Speaking practice with audio comparison
- Mini-test: Identify the correct tone

### 4. Travel Phrases: Greetings & Basics
- Common greetings
- Polite expressions
- Basic questions
- Cultural notes on Thai greetings (wai)
- Mini-test: Complete the dialogue

### 5. Travel Phrases: Getting Around
- Transportation vocabulary
- Asking for directions
- Understanding directions
- Location and prepositions
- Mini-test: Translate phrases

### 6. Travel Phrases: Food & Dining
- Ordering food
- Common Thai dishes
- Expressing preferences
- Numbers and prices
- Mini-test: Restaurant dialogue

### 7. Travel Phrases: Shopping & Bargaining
- Shopping vocabulary
- Asking about prices
- Bargaining phrases
- Numbers and currency
- Mini-test: Bargaining scenario

### 8. Travel Phrases: Emergencies & Health
- Medical vocabulary
- Asking for help
- Explaining problems
- Important locations
- Mini-test: Emergency situation

### 9. Cultural Notes
- Thai customs and etiquette
- Do's and don'ts
- Festivals and holidays
- Mini-test: Cultural awareness quiz

### 10. Resources for Further Learning
- Recommended apps
- Websites
- Books
- YouTube channels
- Practice opportunities

## Interactive Elements
- Audio pronunciation for all Thai words and phrases
- Clickable Thai characters with pronunciation
- Flip cards for vocabulary
- Drag and drop exercises
- Multiple choice mini-tests
- Progress tracking
- Visual feedback for correct/incorrect answers

## Visual Design Elements
- Thai temple-inspired header
- Tropical leaf motifs for section dividers
- Buddhist-inspired decorative elements
- Thai flag colors as accents
- Images of Thailand to illustrate cultural concepts
